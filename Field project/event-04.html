Event Details

TANDAV is an energetic cultural performance event celebrating rhythm, movement, and expression. Participants showcase their talent through powerful dance performances, creativity, and stage presence in an electrifying competition.

How to Register

  • Fill the registration form below with all required details.
  • Click “Send OTP” and verify your registration.
  • Once verified, you will appear in the participants’ list.

Venue Details

College Cultural Auditorium, Main Stage Arena. Reporting time is 9:00 AM on event day.

How Event Validates

  • Participants perform live before judges.
  • Evaluation based on performance, creativity, and stage presence.
  • Top 3 performers are declared winners and notified via email & mobile.
